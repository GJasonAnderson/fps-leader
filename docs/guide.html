Fooled

Week 1: What Lies Are You Believing?

Life / Intro Questions (Choose one or two)

What are you looking forward to the most this upcoming Summer? (BRING UP Movement Conference)

Have you ever had someone lie to you and you believed it for a while until you found out it was a lie?

Do you remember the first time you lied? How did you feel?

Connection Question

Have you ever felt like you needed to earn someone's love (You had to do something for them in order for them to love you)?

Sermon in a Sentence

Nothing changes God's love, God's love changes everything.

Scripture

Psalm 136:26

"Give thanks to the God of heaven. His love endures forever."

What do you think "HIS love endures forever" means? Do you think it's possible for him to stop loving us?

Discussion Questions

Have you had a time where you let your friend down but they didn't hold it against you and still accepted you?

Why do you think it is so difficult for us to believe that God never stops loving us?

Life Application

We want students walking away with 1 or 2 things they are going to do SOON to apply what they discussed.

Who is one person you can show Gods unconditional love to this week?

The next time we sin against God, what is one thing we can do to run towards Him, instead of away from Him?

Take It Out

Pray specifically that we would see the truth about God's love and that we would follow his model of love towards others.

Ask your students if there are any of them that are considering being baptized. Encourage students who are not baptized to take the next step.

Explain to your students what FPS Easter is! Encourage them to bring their parents and friends NEXT WEEK is Student Easter!! Explain this is the PERFECT night to bring their friends and parents to see them get saved!
