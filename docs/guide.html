March Madness

4th Quarter - Power Play - The Invite

Life / Intro Questions (Choose one or two)

What was the best part of your week or what great story do you have from this past week?

What is the best thing you've ever been invited to?

Have you ever found out that something was going on and you didn't get invited? How did that feel?

Connection Question

Do you invite people to be a part of what God is doing in your life?

Scripture

Romans 15:7

Therefore welcome one another as Christ has welcomed you, for the glory of God.

What do you think God is saying to us in this verse? Why is God intentional about calling us out in the way we bring others in?

What happens we invite others to be a part of what God is doing?

Discussion Questions

How did Christ welcome you in? Do you welcome people to be a part of what God is doing in your life the same way Christ welcomed you?

What keeps you from inviting people to meet Jesus?

What does God consider the greatest initiation? Do you hold the invitation into a relationship with God as the best thing you've ever been invited to? Why or why not?

Life Application

We want students walking away with 1 or 2 things they are going to do SOON to apply what they discussed.

What is God doing in your life right now that you are excited about inviting someone to be a part of?

Who can you invite this week? When will you do it?

Take It Out

Close small group out with prayer requests. Pray specifically for the group and connect prayer back to the purpose of the group guide.

After prayer, be sure to remind them of MOVEMENT and THE WEEKEND!
