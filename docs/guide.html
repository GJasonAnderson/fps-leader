Week 2

Life / Intro Questions (Choose one or two)

  • What are you excited about doing this month? (Bring up SKO)
  • Have you ever ended up in a bad place because you were with the wrong people? Explain.

Connection Question

  • Where are areas in your life where you find success? What are some areas you struggle/fail?

Sermon in a Sentence

  • Only Jesus can lead us because only Jesus laid down His life for us.

Scripture

  • John 10:7-11 - So Jesus said again, “I assure you: I am the door of the sheep. All who came before Me are thieves and robbers, but the sheep didn’t listen to them. I am the door. If anyone enters by Me, he will be saved and will come in and go out and find pasture. A thief comes only to steal and to kill and to destroy. I have come so that they may have life and have it in abundance. I am the good shepherd. The good shepherd lays down his life for the sheep.”
  • What is Jesus saying in these scriptures?

Discussion Questions

  • Why do you think we often feel short changed when we fully submit our lives to Jesus and what He has to offer?
  • Personally, what do you think is most challenging when trusting that Jesus will provide for our everyday and our eternity?

Life Application

We want students walking away with 1 or 2 things they are going to do SOON to apply what they discussed.

  • What personal steps can you take this week to surround yourself around God fearing people and also be a person of true faith?
  • How can you help someone else to see Jesus as the good shepherd so that they may live a fulfilled life in Jesus’ name?

Take It Out

  • Close small group out with prayer requests.
  • Pray specifically for the upcoming week that your students work to develop their relationship with Jesus so they can go out and bring people to Jesus.
  • After prayer, be sure to remind them of SKO, Movement and THE WEEKEND!
